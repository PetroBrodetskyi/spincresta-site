Responsible Gambling

At SpinCresta, we are committed to promoting safe and responsible gaming. Gambling should be enjoyable, not harmful.

What is Responsible Gambling?

Responsible gambling means playing in a controlled way, understanding the risks involved, and using available tools to prevent harm or addiction. It is a shared responsibility between players, operators, and regulators.

Key Guidelines for Safe Gambling

  • Set strict budget and time limits before you start playing.
  • Never gamble when you are upset, stressed, tired or under the influence of alcohol or substances.
  • Understand the odds and rules of the games you play.
  • Treat gambling as entertainment, not as a way to make money.
  • Never chase losses - accept them and stop in time.
  • Use self-assessment tools regularly to check your gambling habits.

Tools & Features for Responsible Play

Reputable online casinos provide various responsible gambling tools:

  • Deposit limits (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Loss limits
  • Session / time limits
  • Reality checks / pop-up reminders
  • Cooling-off periods (temporary breaks)
  • Self-exclusion (temporary or permanent)

Signs of Problem Gambling

If you recognize several of these signs, it may be time to seek help:

  • Spending more money or time on gambling than planned
  • Feeling anxious, irritable or restless when trying to stop
  • Lying to family or friends about gambling
  • Gambling to escape problems or relieve negative emotions
  • Neglecting work, family or social responsibilities

Help & Support Resources

Here are some trusted international and regional organizations:

  • United States: 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537) - 24/7 confidential helpline
  • National Council on Problem Gambling: ncpgambling.org
  • Gamblers Anonymous: gamblersanonymous.org - worldwide support groups
  • BeGambleAware (UK & international): begambleaware.org
  • GamCare (UK): gamcare.org.uk - helpline & live chat
