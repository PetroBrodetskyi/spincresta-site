Online Gambling in Germany

Online gambling in Germany is fully regulated under the Glücksspielstaatsvertrag (GlüStV 2021). German players have access to a safe, licensed market offering real-money slots, live dealer games, poker and extensive sports betting — especially football.

Regulation and Licensing in Germany

The Joint Gambling Authority of the Federal States (Gemeinsame Glücksspielbehörde der Länder - GGL) oversees licensing. Operators must comply with strict limits (e.g. €1 spin limit on slots, €1,000 monthly deposit cap), player protection, AML/CFT and responsible gambling rules. Choosing a GlüStV-licensed platform ensures legal protection and high safety standards.
