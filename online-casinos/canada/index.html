Online Gambling in Canada

Online gambling in Canada is regulated at the provincial level. Regulation and Licensing in Canada

Each province sets its own rules. Ontario has a fully open market with KGC and AGCO oversight. Other provinces run monopoly models or allow access to provincially-approved sites. Choosing a provincially-licensed platform guarantees legal protection, fairness and player safety.
