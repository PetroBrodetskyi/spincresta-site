Online Gambling in Sweden

Online gambling in Sweden is regulated by the Swedish Gambling Authority (Spelinspektionen). Licensed operators must comply with strict rules for fairness, security, and responsible gaming. Platforms offer real money casino games, live dealers, and sports betting for Swedish residents.

Legal Status

All operators offering online gambling services to Swedish players must hold a license from Spelinspektionen. Licensed platforms provide player protection, secure payment methods, and adhere to advertising restrictions and responsible gambling policies.
