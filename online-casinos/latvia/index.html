Online Gambling in Latvia

Online gambling in Latvia is legal and regulated. Latvian players can access online casinos, sports betting platforms, poker rooms, and other gambling services provided by licensed operators.

Regulation and Licensing

The Latvian gambling market is regulated by the Izložu un azartspēļu uzraudzības inspekcija (IAUI). Licensed operators must comply with strict legal requirements related to player protection, fair play, and financial transparency. As of 2026, gambling tax rates have been increased, with 75% of taxes going to the state budget and 25% to local governments. Operators must be registered capital companies in Latvia with a minimum share capital of 1,400,000 euros.
