Online Gambling in the United States

Online gambling in the USA operates under a complex state-by-state regulatory framework. A handful of states including New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Michigan, West Virginia, Connecticut and Delaware have fully legalized and regulated real-money online casinos and sportsbooks. In most other states, players turn to sweepstakes and social casinos that use virtual currencies such as Gold Coins for fun play and Sweeps Coins for redeemable prizes.

Legal Status Across States

Real-money online casino gaming and sports betting are only legal in states with specific legislation and licensing.
