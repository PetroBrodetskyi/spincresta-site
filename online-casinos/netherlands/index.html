Online Gambling in the Netherlands

Online gambling in the Netherlands is fully regulated since the Remote Gambling Act (KOA) came into force in October 2021. Dutch players have access to a safe, licensed market offering real-money slots, live dealer games, poker and extensive sports betting — especially football.

Regulation and Licensing in the Netherlands

The Kansspelautoriteit (KSA) oversees online gambling. Only operators with a valid KSA license can legally serve Dutch residents. Strict rules cover fairness, player protection, AML/CFT, advertising restrictions and responsible gambling. Choosing a KSA-licensed platform guarantees legal protection and high safety standards.

Popular Games & Betting Markets

Dutch players enjoy slots, roulette, blackjack, live dealer tables and poker. Sports betting focuses on football (Eredivisie, KNVB Cup, UEFA competitions), cycling, speed skating and tennis. Live in-play betting and cash-out are widely available on licensed sites.
