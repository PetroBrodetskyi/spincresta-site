Online Gambling in Argentina

Online gambling in Argentina is regulated at the provincial level. Provinces like Buenos Aires, Mendoza, Córdoba and Santa Fe issue licenses for real-money casinos and sportsbooks. Argentine players enjoy slots, live dealer games, poker and extensive betting markets — especially fútbol.

Regulation and Licensing in Argentina

Gambling is overseen by provincial regulators (e.g. IPJ in Buenos Aires, IJANM in Mendoza). Licensed operators must comply with strict rules on fairness, player protection, AML/CFT and responsible gambling. Choosing a provincially licensed platform guarantees legal protection and high safety standards.
