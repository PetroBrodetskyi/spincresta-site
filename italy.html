Online Gambling in Italy

Online gambling in Italy is fully legal and strictly regulated. Italian players can access online casinos, sports betting platforms, poker rooms, and other gambling services operated by licensed providers.

The Italian gambling market is regulated by the Agenzia delle Dogane e dei Monopoli (ADM). Operators must hold a valid ADM license, ensuring compliance with strict rules on player protection, financial transparency, and fair gaming practices.
