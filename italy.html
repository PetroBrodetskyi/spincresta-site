Online Gambling in Italy

Online gambling in Italy is fully legal and strictly regulated. Italian players have access to a wide range of ADM-licensed online casinos, sportsbooks, poker rooms and bingo sites. The market is one of the most mature in Europe, with strong focus on player protection and fair play.

Regulation and Licensing in Italy

The Agenzia delle Dogane e dei Monopoli (ADM) oversees all gambling activities in Italy. Licensed operators must meet strict standards on fairness, security, AML/CFT and responsible gambling. Many platforms also hold MGA licenses for added trust and EU compliance.

Popular Games & Betting Markets

Italian players enjoy slots, roulette, blackjack, live dealer tables and poker. Sports betting focuses on calcio (Serie A, Coppa Italia, international tournaments), horse racing (Derby Italiano), tennis, motorsports (Formula 1) and basketball. Live in-play betting and cash-out are widely available.

Bonuses and Promotions

Payment Methods for Italian Players
