SpinCresta.com ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit spincresta.com, how we use, share and protect it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

By using our site, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited data, mostly automatically:

  • Technical & Usage Data - IP address, browser type/version, operating system, device type, pages visited, time/date of visit, time spent on pages, referring/exit pages, clickstream data
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies - small files that help us remember preferences, analyze traffic and improve site performance. We use essential cookies (for functionality) and analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics). You can manage/disable cookies via browser settings

  • No Sensitive Data - we do not collect financial details, payment info, health data or other sensitive personal information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use data only for legitimate purposes:

  • To operate, maintain and improve the website
  • To analyze usage trends and optimize content/user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • To detect/prevent technical issues, errors or malicious activity (e.g., spam, bots)
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders or protect rights/safety

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. Sharing occurs only in limited cases:

  • Service Providers - with analytics (Google Analytics), hosting or security providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements - when required by law, subpoena, court order or government request
  • Protection - to protect rights, property or safety of SpinCresta, users or the public
  • Business Transfers - in case of merger, acquisition or sale of assets (with notice where required)

5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, etc.), you may have the right to:

  • Access - request what data we hold about you
  • Correction - ask to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion - request erasure of your data (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Opt-Out - object to processing or restrict it (e.g., marketing, profiling)
  • Data Portability - receive your data in structured format
  • Withdraw Consent - where processing is consent-based

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@spincresta.com. We respond within 30–45 days (depending on jurisdiction).

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (HTTPS encryption, access controls, regular security audits) to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure - use the site at your own risk.

7. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed or stored outside your country (e.g., US, EU servers). We use appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions) to ensure protection.

8. Children's Privacy

Our site is not intended for persons under 18 (or 21 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we have such data, we delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Significant changes may be notified via email or site notice.

10. Contact Us
