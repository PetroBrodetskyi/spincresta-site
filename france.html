Online Gambling in France: Legal Framework

Online gambling in France is strictly regulated. While online casino games such as slots, roulette, and blackjack are prohibited, online sports betting, horse race betting, and poker are legal and regulated by the Autorité Nationale des Jeux (ANJ).

Licensing and Player Protection

All legal online gambling platforms in France must hold a valid ANJ license. Licensed operators are required to comply with strict rules regarding player identification, data protection, and financial transparency. This regulatory oversight ensures a secure and fair gambling environment for French players.

Available Games and Betting Options

French players can legally access sports betting markets covering football, tennis, basketball, and more, as well as online poker rooms and horse race betting platforms. Game fairness and payout rates are closely monitored by the regulator.

Bonuses and Promotions

Licensed French operators offer welcome bonuses and promotional offers within strict regulatory limits. All bonus terms, wagering requirements, and conditions must be clearly displayed to ensure transparency and informed player decisions.

Mobile Gambling in France

Most licensed platforms in France provide optimized mobile websites and apps, allowing players to place bets and play poker seamlessly on smartphones and tablets.

Responsible Gambling

Responsible gambling is a core requirement under French law. Licensed platforms must provide tools such as deposit limits, self-exclusion options, and access to national support organizations to help players maintain control over their gambling activity.

By choosing licensed online gambling platforms in France, players ensure a secure experience that complies fully with national regulations and player protection standards.
